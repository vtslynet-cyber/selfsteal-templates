★ Three Hives Apiary ★

Raw wildflower honey from the Cotswold hills.

We keep eight colonies above a meadow near the village. Nothing is heated. Nothing is filtered through fine mesh. The honey is exactly what the bees made — pollen, propolis and all.

This Season

The spring flow this year was unusually heavy on dandelion and hawthorn, so the early jars are pale gold and almost buttery. The summer extraction is darker — lime blossom, clover, and a fair bit of bramble.

Each jar is labelled with the hive number and harvest date. Stock is limited; once it's gone, it's gone.
