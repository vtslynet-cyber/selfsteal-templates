One Sheet

origami studies · Hanae Tanaka · since 2011
△ △ △ △ △ △ fold

I fold paper. One sheet, no cuts, no glue. Most pieces start as a square 25 centimetres on a side. I've been doing this for fifteen years and I still find new shapes inside the same square.

What I post here are quiet studies — sometimes a bird, sometimes a knot of geometry that doesn't look like anything.
