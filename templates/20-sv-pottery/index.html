Linnea Holm

Stoneware Studio · Göteborg

I make functional pottery for everyday use — bowls, cups, plates, jugs. Each piece is hand-thrown on the wheel, glazed in small batches, and fired in my gas kiln behind the house.

My work tends toward quiet shapes and muted glazes. I'm drawn to the way an old wooden spoon settles into a worn bowl, or how morning light falls across a stack of plates on a shelf.
