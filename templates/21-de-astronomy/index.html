STERNGUCKER

Amateur Astronomy Club · Innsbruck · Founded 1971

We are a small group of amateur astronomers meeting once a month to observe from our dark site above Kühtai (2020 m), thirty kilometres west of the city. Visitors and beginners are warmly welcome.

We share telescopes, binoculars, and warm tea. The site has an old shepherd's hut for shelter when the wind picks up.
