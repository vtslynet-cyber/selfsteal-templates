THREE STONES BREWERY

PORTLAND, MAINE — EST. 2012

A small production brewery on the working waterfront. Beer made by three friends who used to homebrew in a garage and never quite shook the habit.

We brew what we like to drink: balanced, sessionable beers with a strong respect for tradition and occasional flights of fancy.
