Quiet Garden Bonsai

Vancouver Island · since 2003

A small nursery and study garden devoted to bonsai. We grow our own material from seedlings, collected yamadori from the dry interior, and a few specimens given by retiring growers over the years.

We don't ship trees. Visitors are welcome by appointment — bring questions, leave room in your boot in case something follows you home.

In the garden now

Shore pine Pinus contorta, collected 2009 from a sea cliff near Tofino. In training as a literati.
Trident maple Acer buergerianum, started from seed 1997. Twenty-eight years in this pot.
Western juniper Juniperus occidentalis, large yamadori from the Okanagan. Half-deadwood specimen.
Japanese black pine Pinus thunbergii, grown from imported nursery stock in 2002. Classical informal upright.
